2023

Location: University of Colorado Boulder

Class: Engineering Projects

Tools: Arduino, Acrylic, Wood-working, CAD, Laser-cutting, Soldering

In the Engineering Projects class, we were put in teams to invent a product that can solve a problem. Considering a target group of the visually impaired, we designed an electronically controlled measuring cup that can take digital measurements, and adjust accordingly to the input.

Components: Water tight gasket, electronic servo, buttons and digital screen interface, adjustable chuck
