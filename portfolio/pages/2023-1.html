Given the task to create a mock-app design, I used Figma to create an interactive transportation app geared toward the elderly. This required a user-friendly and accessible interface that addresses the specific needs and challenges that older adults may face.

These include:

- Accessibility features

- Companion requests

- Tracking

- Scheduling
