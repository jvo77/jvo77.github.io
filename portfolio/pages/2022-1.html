2022

Location: Community College of Aurora

Class: Art

Tools: Graphite Pencils, Charcoal

In this art course, our class was assigned to create a meaningful art piece. With family in mind, I decided to draw a landscape of my childhood home. I captured this scene of my grandpa doing what he loved most: Gardening. This was one of my favorite art pieces, not only because of the realism, but also because I was able to relive the memory of watching my grandpa water his plants and upkeep our beautiful childhood home.
