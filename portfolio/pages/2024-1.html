Lamps!

2024

Location: University of Colorado Boulder

Class: Form

Tools: Laser-cutting, CAD, Rhino, On-shape, Sketch-up

For our final in the class, we used our CAD and structural design knowledge to create lamps. We were directed to create two lamps made from different materials. I created one lamp using Sketchup and Onshape to create a lantern design. For my second lamp, I used acrylic to create a “flower” designed hanging lamp.
